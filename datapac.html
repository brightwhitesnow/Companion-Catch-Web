    Data Privacy and Consent

    At Companion Catch, we prioritize your privacy and the ethical use of technology. Our "Companion Catch" fall detection system is designed with your safety and confidentiality in mind. Before using our application, we ensure that all individuals whose data might be captured by our system provide their informed consent. We believe in transparency and are committed to clearly communicating how we use, store, and protect the data collected by our system.

    Your video data is used exclusively for the purpose of detecting falls and providing timely assistance. We implement stringent data security measures, including robust encryption and strict access controls, to safeguard your information against unauthorized access. We store this data securely and only for as long as necessary to serve its intended purpose. Rest assured, we do not share your personal data with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law.

    We understand the importance of trust in our relationship with you. Therefore, we are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and ethical conduct in all our operations. If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please feel free to contact us on our Contact Page.
