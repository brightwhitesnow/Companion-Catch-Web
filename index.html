    Companion Catch, is designed to recognize and track falls as they happen using AI technology. If a fall is identified, an alert is sent without delay to a designated computer, ensuring timely assistance. Inspired by personal experiences and driven by the goal to provide an invisible companion to those in need, the app combines advanced computer vision techniques with a deep learning model to offer both safety and peace of mind to users and their loved ones.
