    User Guidelines

    Companion Catch is dedicated to enhancing safety through our advanced fall detection technology. To ensure the responsible and ethical use of our system, we have established these guidelines. Adherence to these guidelines is crucial for maintaining the integrity and trustworthiness of our service.

    Appropriate Use:

    1. Fall Detection and Emergency Assistance:

      Companion Catch is designed to detect falls and provide alerts for emergency assistance. It should be used primarily in environments where this is a critical need, such as in homes for the elderly or healthcare facilities.

    2. Informed Consent:

      Use Companion Catch in settings where all individuals who might be recorded have given their informed consent for monitoring.

    3. Privacy Respect:

      Employ Companion Catch in a manner that respects the privacy of individuals. Avoid using it in private areas like bathrooms unless explicitly consented to for safety reasons.

    4. Data Security:

      Handle all data captured by Companion Catch responsibly, adhering to our stringent data protection and encryption protocols.

    5. Non-Discriminatory Use:

      Ensure that the deployment of Companion Catch does not discriminate against any individual or group.


    Inappropriate Use:

    1. Surveillance and Monitoring:

      Companion Catch is not to be used for general surveillance, monitoring without consent, or any form of spying.

    2. Commercial Exploitation:

      Data or footage captured by Companion Catch must not be used for commercial purposes, such as advertising or selling to third parties, without explicit consent.

    3. Manipulation or Tampering:

      Altering, manipulating, or tampering with Companion Catchâ€™s operation or its data for unauthorized purposes is strictly prohibited.

    4. Discrimination or Bias:

      Companion Catch must not be used in a way that promotes discrimination or bias against any individual or group.

    5. Illegal Activities:

      Companion Catch must not be used for any illegal activities or in ways that contravene local, national, or international laws.


    Compliance and Enforcement:

    • User Responsibility:

      Users are responsible for ensuring their use of Companion Catch aligns with these guidelines.

    • Violation Consequences:

      Violations may result in service suspension or termination, and legal action where applicable.

    • Reporting Misuse:

      If misuse of Companion Catch is encountered or suspected, please report it to us on our Contact Page.


    These guidelines ensure that Companion Catch is used ethically and responsibly. We reserve the right to update these guidelines as necessary. Your cooperation is vital for upholding the ethical standards we value.
